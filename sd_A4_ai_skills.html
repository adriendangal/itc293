Why AI Matters at NexaCore

NexaCore Corporation offers AI integration as a core service. Employees who understand how to use AI tools effectively, and who can explain their value to clients, are directly aligned with the company's strategic direction.

I have hands-on experience using AI tools to build a product, conduct research, write outreach content, and improve workflows. This is not theoretical knowledge — I have applied AI to a real project targeting paying clients.

AI Receptionist Business Project

My most significant AI project is an AI receptionist product for small Australian businesses. The product uses an AI phone agent to answer incoming calls, respond to common questions, and capture appointment requests — reducing the need for a human receptionist.

Target industries included:

  • Dental clinics
  • Real estate agencies
  • Legal and accounting firms
  • Trade businesses (plumbers, electricians)

This project required me to understand AI workflow tools, build a functional product, develop a go-to-market strategy, and pitch the value proposition to non-technical business owners.
